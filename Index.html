Solo Bag — Strategy
Deep Dive

End-to-end thinking: audience research → content pillars → execution → design output.

🏷 Brand & context

Solo Bag is an Ethiopian fashion brand specializing in premium leather bags. Target audience: urban women aged 18–35 who value quality, aesthetics, and self-expression. Key platforms: Instagram and TikTok.

CONTENT PILLAR DISTRIBUTION

✍️ Hook writing strategy

Instagram hooks lead with identity and aspiration ("A bag that matches every vibe"). TikTok hooks lead with tension or curiosity ("Never clean suede like this..."). CTAs are matched to content type: saves for educational, DMs for promotional, comments for engagement posts.

🎨 Design execution

In addition to the strategic calendar, produced a Canva Instagram graphic for Solo Bag's Brown Leather Bag collection — demonstrating the ability to execute both strategy and creative output for the same brand end-to-end.

"The most effective content strategy isn't about posting more — it's about posting with purpose. Every piece of content should serve one of three goals: build trust, provide value, or drive action."
